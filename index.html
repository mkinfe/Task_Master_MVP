A Simple and Intuitive Task Management

Description

Welcome to TaskMaster, a simple and intuitive task management application designed to help users organize their tasks effectively. With TaskMaster, you can easily add, edit, and delete tasks, prioritize them with color-coded labels, and track your progress.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for adding, editing, and deleting tasks
  • Prioritization of tasks with color-coded labels
  • Ability to mark tasks as complete and view completed tasks
